The treetops, the chimneys, the snowbed stories, winter grey<br/>

Blue bayous, Decembers, moon through a dragonfly's wings<br/>

那些我曾度过无眠的夜在哪里<br/>
Before the years take me<br/>

I wish to see<br/>
我希望能看见<br/>
